Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta al dente.
  2. Fry the bacon until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and mix with grated Parmesan.
  4. Combine everything and add black pepper.

Enjoy your meal!
